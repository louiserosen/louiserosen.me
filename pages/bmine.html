Bmine is an online tool that offers a 100% customisable service to discover Brussels. Based on thumbnails classified into different themes, the user chooses to map a series of activities.

The user will be able to either print a flyer with the selected journey or store the details on a mobile device.

Details of places to visit, local transport routes and annotation facilities are included in the journey details.
