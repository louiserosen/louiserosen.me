Creation of a new online tool that offers a 100% customizable service to discover Brussels. Based on thumbnails classified into different themes, the user chooses to map a series of activities. He is able to either print a flyer with the selected journey or store the details on a mobile device.
More details about places to visit, local transport routes and other facilities are then available in the journey details.

Collaboration with Carole Pes and Ange Tchendo
