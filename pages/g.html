Vitamine g
—

Themed project about : «What is graphic design?».
Creation of a new line of pharmaceutical products called 'Vitamine g' and designed to fight the evils of graphic design. Loss of creativity, insomnia, stress, sleepless nights and brain overheating are depicted as playthings provided with a form and instructions.

Collaboration with Lena Vancanneyt

La Cambre, 2014
